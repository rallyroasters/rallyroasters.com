Privacy Policy

Clear, simple policies for our custom coffee fundraiser website and organizer inquiries.

Rally Roasters respects your privacy. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Information We Collect

When you submit a fundraiser inquiry form, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • School or organization information
  • Fundraiser details you choose to share

How We Use Information

We use submitted information to respond to fundraiser inquiries, communicate about fundraiser setup and logistics, provide customer support, and improve the Rally Roasters experience.

Information Sharing

We don’t sell personal information. Information may be shared with trusted service providers only when necessary to operate fundraiser campaigns, process payments, or fulfill coffee orders.

Payment Processing

Payments may be processed through third-party providers such as Stripe or Formspree. Rally Roasters does not store full payment information directly on our servers.

Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies or analytics tools to understand website traffic and improve site performance.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect submitted information, but no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to info@rallyroasters.com.
